About Us

Grace and Truth Full Gospel Pentecostal Church was co-founded in Denver Colorado in the mid 90's by Theodis and Tastonga Moore. Started by the idea and hope of helping our community with acts of service and guidance through the teachings of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, we continue to demonstrate our belief by showing how one can have a sprit-filled and empowered life by connection with the Holy Spirit and God. The Grace and Truth Chruch continues to be a staple in the community by adopting the model to help whoever we can, however we can, any way we can.
